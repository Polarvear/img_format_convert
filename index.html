Browser support

Modern Chrome/Edge/Firefox/Safari can decode WEBP & AVIF. Unsupported browsers will show a load error.

How to convert WEBP or AVIF to PNG (fast & offline)

  1. Drag and drop WEBP/AVIF images, or click Choose files.
  2. Click Convert on each card or Convert all.
  3. Download the generated PNG files. That’s it!
FAQ · Is this converter safe for sensitive images?

Yes. Conversion happens locally in your browser via Canvas APIs. No uploads, no servers, and no database are involved.
